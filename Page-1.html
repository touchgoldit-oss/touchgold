WHO WE ARE ?

At Touch Gold, we believe that jewellery is more than just an accessory—it serves as an expression of beauty, confidence, and timeless elegance. Based in Surat, the heart of India’s jewellery and diamond craftsmanship, we specialize in premium gold jewellery, diamond jewellery, and silver jewellery that seamlessly blend traditional artistry with contemporary design.

As trusted manufacturers, we are committed to delivering superior quality through precise detailing, premium materials, and enduring brilliance. Every creation, including our exquisite bridal jewellery, reflects our focus on craftsmanship, innovation, and lasting value.

Our collections feature finely crafted rings, bracelets, bangles, pendants, and custom jewellery designs, thoughtfully created to enhance every occasion—from everyday elegance to weddings and special celebrations
